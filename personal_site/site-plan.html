Overview

Purpose

This site is your place to go if you are seeking activities and events in your area. Users can view featured attractions in their area or filter activities by categories and tags such as energy level, budget, and time limit. Users can then select an activity or attraction to view specific details.

Audience

This website is designed to be inclusive and relevant to people of all ages and backgrounds in the Rexburg Idaho area.

Dynamic elements

When a user applies filters Javascript will be used to retrieve search results with tags that match the filters. When the "more info" button is selected on a search result, a modal will appear on the screen and javascript will display data associated with the correct activity.

Board Games
	Catan: Players trade and settle resources to dominate the island in this classic strategy game.
	Secret Hitler: A hidden roles game where players deceive and deduce to uncover the secret fascists or stop them.
	Exploding Kittens: A chaotic card game of luck and strategy where players avoid drawing the dreaded kitten card.
	Scythe: A visually stunning game set in an alternate-history 1920s, focused on conquest, resource management, and mech battles.
	Unstable Unicorns: Players build unicorn armies while sabotaging others in this competitive card game.
	Terraforming Mars: Compete to transform Mars into a habitable planet by managing resources and strategic card play.
	Azul: A tile-laying game where players create beautiful mosaics while managing limited resources.
	Gloomhaven: A cooperative campaign game with rich storytelling and evolving gameplay.
	Ticket to Ride: A classic game of building train routes across countries while balancing risk and reward.
	Pandemic: Players work together to stop global outbreaks in this cooperative survival game.
	Taskmaster Board Game: Inspired by the TV show, players complete absurd challenges for points and laughs.
	Codenames: A team-based word game where players identify their agents through clever clues.
	Dixit: A storytelling game where players interpret whimsical artwork to score points.
	Ark Nova: Players build and manage modern zoos in this strategic and highly thematic game.
	Cascadia: A puzzle tile-placement game about creating harmonious habitats for wildlife.
	Root: A war game of woodland creatures vying for control, blending strategy with asymmetric play.
	Scrawl: A hilarious party game where bad drawings and wacky interpretations bring laughs.
	Corks: A fast-paced family game where players grab tokens to match their cards.
	Carcassonne: Players build medieval landscapes with tiles, scoring by completing cities, roads, and fields.
